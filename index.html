Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our loan services.

What are the requirements to get a loan?

You need to be 18 years or older, have a valid Kenyan ID, and an active M-Pesa account registered in your name.

How long does it take to receive the funds?

Once approved, the funds are disbursed to your M-Pesa account within minutes.

What is the maximum amount I can borrow?

New customers can borrow up to Ksh 5,000. Returning customers can access up to Ksh 20,000 based on their repayment history.

Is there a penalty for early repayment?

No, we encourage early repayment and do not charge any penalties for paying back your loan early.
